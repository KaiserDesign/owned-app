Original idea based on:

\*\*Responsibilities in that of things:\*\* 



\- There might be an array of things to do before obtained, or use. \*(E.g. Licenses \& certificates)\*

\- Once obtained there may be special 1st time use instructions, or even things that should be done not listed in the instructions. \*(E.g. assembly)\*

\- If thing was used, then not used for a longer than standard duration of time, this may need special steps before use again. \*(E.g. flushing a Coffee machine with vinegar to clear mold.)\*

\- If this can be used more than once, and is used more aggressively than standard cases, thing may need additional actions. \*(e.g. Replacement of O-ring)\*

\- If this can be used more than once, and is used under the normal level of stress, thing may need additional actions. \*(E.g. Greasing a machine.)\*

\- Over duration of life, thing may have standard and recommend actions, some mandatory. \*(E.g. Car gas, or oil changes.)\*

\- When applied under special conditions thing may need special actions \*(e.g. )\*

\- Once the thing has served your purpose, there are likely steps to take when disowning said thing \*(e.g. title transfer (relinquishing of ownership))\*

\- The absence of said thing may also have an effect on its previous surrounding variables. An adaption or accommodation may need to be made. \*(e.g. )\*
